About Warboard V2

What is Warboard V2?

Warboard V2 is a free online tabletop war game simulator built to let friends and family play miniature wargames together remotely — no downloads, no subscription, no setup required. Create a session, share the link, and battle across a fully scaled 72″ × 48″ virtual board from anywhere in the world.

How to Play

  1. Create a session from the lobby. Choose Open or Password-locked.
  2. Share the session with your opponent so they can join.
  3. During the Setup phase, Player 1 can place terrain blocks. Both players add their units using the sidebar.
  4. When ready, Player 1 clicks Start Game. Terrain locks and the active phase begins.
  5. Players alternate turns by clicking End Turn. Move your units, roll dice, and track HP during your turn.
  6. Use the Measure tool to check range, and watch for green / red rings that show line-of-sight to nearby units.
